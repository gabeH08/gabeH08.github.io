Background

This dish is a classical European dish which is traced back to ancient Rome. My grandmother used to make flan when I went to Argentina for christmas, and the way she makes it was so similar to the way my mom makes it. I chose this dish because I love eating flan and my mom in my opinion makes the best flan, but she makes it during winter season. My family enjoys eating her flan dish, I am truly grateful that my mom makes this type of dish for our family

Instructions

HOW MUCH TIME TO MAKE FLAN
Time?
Preperation: 15-20 minutes
Cooking: 40 minutes
Eating: less than 1 second

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 2 cups of normal milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar

Directions

How to make?
Step 1: First put 1/4 of the sugar in a saucepan and heat it until it melts and the caramel forms.
Step 2: Next pour it into a bowl.
Step 3: In a blender put 8 eggs, 2 tablespoons of vanilla essence,and 14oz can of condensed milk.
Step 4: Mix the ingredientes for a few minutes.
Step 5: Then pour the ingredientes into the bowl where we poured the caramel.
Step 6: Put it in the oven with another larger bowl underneath with some water inside the bowl.
Step 7: Lastly cook it for 40 minutes at 360 Fahreheit.
