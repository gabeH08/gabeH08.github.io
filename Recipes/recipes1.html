Background

Flan is a classic European dessert traced back to ancient Rome. I chose this dish because I love eating flan, and my mom, in my opinion, makes the best flan especially during the winter season. My family enjoys her flan, and I am truly grateful that she makes this delicious dish for us.

How Much Time to Make Flan

Preparation and Cooking Time
Time
Preparation: 15 to 20 minutes
Cooking: 40 minutes
Eating: Less than 1 second

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1 can (14 oz) of condensed milk
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Instructions

How to Make Flan
Step 1: Put 1/4 of the sugar in a saucepan and heat until melted and caramelized.
Step 2: Carefully pour the caramel into a baking bowl.
Step 3: In a blender, combine 8 eggs, 2 tablespoons vanilla extract, and 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk.
Step 4: Blend the ingredients for a few minutes until smooth.
Step 5: Pour the mixture into the bowl with the caramel.
Step 6: Place the bowl in a larger baking dish filled with water.
Step 7: Bake for 40 minutes at 360 Fahrenheit (182 Celsius).
